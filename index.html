About Us

Don Bosco School(Nilgiri) is a group of schools including a Nursery, Primary, Junior and Senior Secondary Schools, located in Biratnagar 1, Pokhariya, Nepal. It was founded by Don Bosco and was established in June 2005. Don Bosco School set on hundreds of square meters of lush green land in the middle of Biratnagar 1, it is a breath of fresh air. The school has all that parents need to ensure qualitative, affordable and morally sound education for their children.
